HealthTensor

Automating documentation, so clinicians can get back to patients.

The Problem

Doctors are burned out on documentation.

Physicians spend 50% of their day reviewing data and documenting on the computer and less than a third of their time with patients. This leads to physician and patient dissatisfaction.

However, without documentation physicians and hospital systems cannot get paid. Physicians sacrifice documentation quality to spend more time with patients, but vague or incomplete documentation leads to an estimated $10B in lost revenue each year.

The Solution

Documenting with AI.

HealthTensor develops artificially intelligent systems to automate documentation for physicians. Starting with hospital patients, our technology mines the medical record for pertinent information, assists in diagnosing key conditions, and generates proper documentation.

By integrating into the workflow, HealthTensor can provide trusted, evidence-backed notes directly to physicians to save time, reduce liability, and optimize appropriate reimbursements.
